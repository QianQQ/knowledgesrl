Welcome to Robert Swier SRL Baseline's documentation!
=====================================================

Our objective is to reproduce (Swier & Stevenson, 2005) results for recent
versions of resources and NLP tools (MST Parser, latest VerbNet, FrameNet and
VN-FN mappings).
